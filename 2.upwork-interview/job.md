Sr. Business Process Consultant
Management Consulting Posted Feb 20, 2026
Source-to-Pay (S2P) / Finance & Supply Chain

The Team
The Customer Outcomes team at ServiceNow works with customers to help them achieve their
business outcomes by providing implementation and advisory services. As part of the Customer
Outcomes team, you will work with our customers and partners to drive consumption, adoption, andNcustomer satisfaction and ultimately help our customers grow their business on the ServiceNow platform by getting them to see the value of their ServiceNow investment.

The Role
The Sr. Business Process Consultant for Source-to-Pay (S2P) is the functional and process expert
within a customer engagement team. This role combines deep expertise in procurement, finance,
and supply chain processes with consulting skills to guide clients through the implementation of
ServiceNow’s Source-to-Pay (S2P) and Finance & Supply Chain (FSC) solutions. The successful
candidate will consult with customers, transition business requirements into configuration
requirements, and drive best-practice-based solutions that accelerate customer business outcomes and enhance operational efficiency.

What you get to do in this role
Process Consulting & Customer Engagement:
• Be the process expert in how to best support Finance, Supply Chain & Procurement
functions using ServiceNow S2P/FSC products and best practices, focused on configuration
vs. customisation
• Drive FSC-specific process definition, re-engineering, improvement, and gap analysis of
current/future-state processes during workshops with key customer sponsors and
stakeholders
• Lead customers in their efforts to leverage ServiceNow’s Source-to-Pay standard capabilities
to improve procurement, accounts payable, and supplier management processes
• Lead customer design workshops focused on S2P/FSC functionality, including Source to
Contract, Supplier Management, Procure to Pay (P2P), Accounts Payable (AP), Accounts
Receivable (AR), Order to Cash (O2C), Record to Report (R2R) and some other Finance and
Source to Pay workflows.
• Guide and advocate for the customer’s needs throughout the engagement
Requirements & Solution Design
• Own support of key customer process owners and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to obtain
business requirements using ServiceNow standard materials and collateral
• Guide customers in completing required documentation such as business requirement
workbooks for functional aspects of the solution
• Draft functionality-focused user stories and acceptance criteria, supporting customers in
reviewing and approving them
• Define and architect solutions at a detailed level, ensuring alignment with clients’ business
needs and technological environments
Project Delivery & Quality:
• Act as a lead member of the overall engagement project team focused on delivering
successful and substantive customer outcomes
• Support internal unit testing of developed solutions during the development stage, in
partnership with Technical Resources
• Prepare all customer-facing deliverables focused on process
• Serve as an escalation point for functional and process-related issues, implementing
efficiencies and driving resolution of critical path challenges
• Juggle multiple and complex projects/initiatives simultaneously

Collaboration & Mentoring:
• Engage with ServiceNow product teams to provide feedback and insights on new features,
capabilities, and best practices
• Provide training and mentoring to other members of the ServiceNow delivery team and
partner ecosystem upon request
• Promote continuous improvement practices for delivery and engagement materials

Qualifications
In order to be successful in this role, we need someone who has:
Experience:
• A minimum of 5 years of consulting experience for complex, global organisations, with a
focus on finance, procurement, or supply chain transformation
• Proven track record in designing and implementing S2P/FSC or related solutions, with a solid
understanding of industry-specific workflows, use cases, compliance requirements, and best
practices
• Experience in defining and deploying future-state procurement, supply chain and finance
processes, identifying solutions from a people, process, and technology perspective with a
focused prowess on technology implementation
• Solid experience in converting business requirements to configuration requirements by
authoring user stories that support Agile Development
• Experience in leveraging or critically thinking about how to integrate AI into work processes,
decision-making, or problem-solving, including using AI-powered tools, automating
workflows, or analysing AI-driven insights.
Domain Knowledge:
• Strong understanding of Source-to-Contract, Procure-to-Pay, Supplier Management, and
related finance and supply chain domains.
• Familiarity with leading ERP and procurement systems such as SAP Ariba, Coupa, Oracle, or
similar platforms
• Demonstrated ability to influence and consult (providing options with pros, cons, and risks)
in a Finance & Procurement / Shared Services environment, providing thought leadership to
sponsors and stakeholders.
Skills:
• Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal) with strong presentation and
facilitation skills (proficiency in Visio, Word, and PowerPoint)
• Strong interpersonal skills, customer-centric attitude, and ability to deal with cultural
diversity
• Ability to influence senior leaders and stakeholders, providing clear recommendations that
address business and process challenges in Finance & Procurement contexts
• Proven team player and team builder
• Experience in providing Knowledge-Centred Service/Support using technologies
Languages
• English – Fluent (required)
• German – Native or bilingual proficiency (optional) lessMore/Less about
